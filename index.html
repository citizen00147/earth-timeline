TIMELINE

Scroll right to explore the geological timeline of Earth. Numbers are written as mya (millon years ago)

4,600

Earth is formed from clumps of dust left over by the sun.

4,500

Dense metals sink to the center of the Earth, forming the core. The outside layer cools and hardens, forming the crust.

4,400

Volcanic activity releases water vapour into the air. After it cools, it falls back down as rain and forms the first oceans.

3,850

The very first organisms appear.

1,500

Oxygen is created from blue-green algae through photosynthesis. Oxygen reacts with iron and sinks to the bottom of the ocean. About 1,500 millon years ago the iron runs out and the oxygen trapped in rock layers under the ocean is released back into the atmosphere.

700

The first animals evolve.

530

The first fish evolve.

400

Oxygen in the atmosphere reacts and forms ozone, which creates a layer that shields life on Earth from harmful rays coming from space. This shield allows plant life to thrive.

350

Plants provide a source of food for primative amphibians, the first to venture on land. Reptiles soon follow.

225

The first dinosaurs evolve from lizards

65

Meteors colliade with Earth and start a chain-reaction: earthquakes, tsunamis, and volcanic eruptions, throwing dust in the air which blocks the sunlight and prevents plant life from growing. Food chains collapse and the dinosaurs go extinct.

0.13

Homo Sapiens evolve in Africa from earlier humans. They left Africa around 35,000 years ago to explore other lands.
